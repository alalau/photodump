Explorations at the intersection of technical surrender and presence. By capturing every image using entirely automatic camera settings, one deliberately bypasses the manual calibration of light and focus, living fully within the unfolding moment. Prioritizing the candid rhythm of life over technical perfection, Automatic invites viewers to witness a world seen through an attentive yet unencumbered lens.
